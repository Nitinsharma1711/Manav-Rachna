About Conference

The Department of Computer Applications at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and studies is pleased to announce the upcoming conference titled "Innovations and Future Trends in Computer Applications,". This conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to discuss the latest advancements and future directions in the field of computer applications.

Conference Highlights

The conference will feature keynote speeches, panel discussions, and paper presentations on a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity and Data Privacy
  • Software Development and Engineering
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Emerging Technologies in Computer Applications

We invite you to join us for this exciting event to share your research, exchange ideas, and network with peers from around the world on

17 May 2025

About School of Computer Applications

The School of Computer Applications was established with a focus on imparting the fundamental concepts both theoretically and practically. Keeping in view the growth in industry and increasing demand for computer professionals, the department offers some industry associated courses such as MCA, BCA with various specializations in association with Microsoft, B.Sc.(Information Technology) with Dual Specialization and Ph.D.programmes. To meet the needs of industry and R&D organizations, the course curriculum has been carefully customized to meet the rising global outlook and focuses on forthcoming innovations in the field of Computer Applications.The Department of Computer Applications is actively engaged in fostering global connections through strategic international partnerships and collaborations and MOU’s. Our department is proud to host a diverse and vibrant community of international students from around the globe.Our international students bring unique perspectives and ideas that enhance our research, discussions, and collaborative projects.

About Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS)

Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS, formerly MRIU- a NAAC Accredited ‘A++’ Grade Institution), ‘Deemed-to be-University’ under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956, offers programs from Graduate to Doctoral level that are in tune with the market dynamics. The Institution has been conferred with 5-Star rating for Teaching, Employability, Academic Development, Facilities, Social Responsibility and Inclusiveness by QS Rating System. the latest NIRF 2024 rankings, MRIIRS Faridabad ranks #92 under the category 'University'.
